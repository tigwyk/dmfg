What is Re:TRADE? A primer for the uninitiated

Re:TRADE aims to fill a void.

Currently in the Ingress community, when you want to trade items with another player you're often forced to request the needed equipment on various chat/comms and hope that someone a) reads your request, b) has the equipment you need, c) plays in an area you can easily get to.

Due to this, I've created this site to offer a marketplace-style listing of requests or offers for equipment to trade, along with the player's in-game name and area they often play in. This should help increase productive gear swapping and reduce the headaches caused by trying to coordinate with other players.

Feedback is greatly appreciated, my in-game name is @tigwyk, drop me a line!
